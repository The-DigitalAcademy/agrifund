About AgriFund

In South Africa, there are approximately two million small-scale farmers compared to 35 000 commercial growers. Small-scale farmers rely on their land to feed their families while selling and trading any extra produce they have left.

  If provided with the opportunity to acquire business funding, they will be able to grow their farming activities to produce a greater crop yield that can be sold to the community. However, most small-scale farmers don’t have bookkeeping records which automatically disqualifies them from business funding.

  AgriFund’s purpose is to create a platform that will allow small-scale farmers to bookkeep data that can be used to build a financial portfolio needed in applying for business funding.
